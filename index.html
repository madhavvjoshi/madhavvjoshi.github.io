About Me

I lead the Transmission and High Voltage systems for Team Genesis 16 Motorsports (SAEINDIA eBAJA 2026). My work spans complete drivetrain architecture — including pulley reduction, 2-stage EN36 gearbox design, differential integration, and custom CV joint development.
